Common questions

Does AshVault require an account or internet?
No. AshVault works offline. Progress is stored on your device.

How do I reset my progress?
Delete and reinstall the app, or abandon your current run from Settings.

Does the app show ads or in-app purchases?
No. AshVault is free with no ads and no IAP.

Where is my data stored?
Locally on your iPhone or iPad using iOS UserDefaults. We do not operate a cloud sync service.
